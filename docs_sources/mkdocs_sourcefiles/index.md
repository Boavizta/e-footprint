# e-footprint

**Model your digital service's footprint. Decide where to act.**

e-footprint is an open-source tool, hosted by [Boavizta](https://www.boavizta.org/en), that models the environmental impact of a digital service, web or edge, so teams can compare scenarios and prioritize ecodesign effort *before* they build.

Ecodesigning a service is less about "what could I do?" than "what should I do first, and what impact would that have?". Best practices tell you what's possible and measurement tells you what's happening today; e-footprint adds the missing leg: a model of how your service is actually used, in real volumes and geographies, that lets you simulate a change before you make it and see where effort actually pays off. The full argument is in [Why e-footprint?](why_efootprint.md).

**Who it's for:** product teams and sustainability leads whose decisions shape a digital service's or device fleet's impact, from engineers, designers, PMs and architects to the hardware and IoT teams on the edge side. Approachable for non-code roles, with depth available on demand.
