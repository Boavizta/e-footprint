    EdgeComputerRAMComponent

    Internal EdgeRAMComponent created automatically by an EdgeComputer. Its capacity, power, and lifespan are pulled from the parent computer.

    Params

    name

    A human readable description of the object.

    carbon_footprint_fabrication_per_unit

    Embodied carbon emitted to manufacture one unit of this RAM bank.

    Unit: kilogram.

    Fixed by EdgeComputerRAMComponent to 0.0 kg — not configurable.

    power_per_unit

    Electrical power drawn by one fully-loaded RAM unit.

    Unit: watt.

    Fixed by EdgeComputerRAMComponent to 0.0 W — not configurable.

    idle_power_per_unit

    Electrical power drawn by one idle RAM unit.

    Unit: watt.

    Fixed by EdgeComputerRAMComponent to 0.0 W — not configurable.

    nb_of_units

    Number of identical RAM units in the component.

    Unit: dimensionless.

    Fixed by EdgeComputerRAMComponent to 1.0 — not configurable.
